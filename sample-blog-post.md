## The 5-Minute Red Flag Checklist

Before you sign ANY contractor quote, check for these warning signs:

**🚨 IMMEDIATE RED FLAGS:**

- [ ] Vague line items like "labor and materials"
- [ ] Project management >15% of total cost  
- [ ] No material specifications or quantities
- [ ] Prices 25%+ above regional averages
- [ ] Missing permit costs or inspections
- [ ] No timeline or milestone payments
- [ ] Upfront payment >10% of total cost

**✅ GREEN FLAGS (Good Signs):**

- [ ] Detailed material specifications
- [ ] Itemized labor by trade
- [ ] References from recent projects
- [ ] Proper licensing and insurance
- [ ] Fixed-price contract with change order process
